Learn Kabaddi Raiding Skills

Raid is the focal point of Kabaddi. A couple of good raiders can change the whole scenario of the match within minutes with their superior techniques and tactics. Since more points can be scored through raids, the raider is always in the lime light.

Rules of Kabaddi

Before you start playing a game, learn the rules & also the specific aspects of it. What can your opponent do? The better you know the rules of the game, the more chances you have to win and can be the recipient of public accolades depending on the quality of his/her raids.

Learn Kabaddi defense Skills (ankle hold)

Ankle hold is an individual defensive skill in Kabaddi. This is a counter skill used by defensive players against leg thrusts and foot touches by raiders during an attack or raid. When the bonus line game was in vogue, this skill was a must for the corner players because every raider would try to cross the Bonus line in the corner zone.

Kabaddi Tackle Skills

The chain block in Kabaddi is a defence skill used as a strategy to capture tall & heavy players. This skill is classified into three types i.e., chain holds by corner/centre players, running & following chain holds. Each of these types of chain holds can be applied depending on the situation and have different hand grips for the best results.
