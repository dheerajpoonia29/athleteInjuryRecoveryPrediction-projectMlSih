Toe Injury

More Details

One such injury is "turf toe," something that happens in football or other sports when the toe is forcefully bent in the wrong way
Symptoms:Throbbing pain,hear the bone break,swelling at the break.
Recovery:4 to 6 weeks
Dietary Intake :calcium and vitamin D,milk, cheese, and yogurt


Hamstring

More Details

A hamstring injury is a strain or tear to the tendons or large muscles at the back of the thigh.
Symptoms:Sudden and severe pain,Pain in the back of the thigh,Tenderness,Bruising
Recovery:weeks or months
Dietary Intake : Protein and potassium,anthocyanins (plant compounds),omega-3 fats, Plain or vanilla yogurt topped with blueberries, grilled salmon over wild rice, or 1-2 cups cooked broccoli


Knee Niggle

More Details

The cartilage in the knee gradually wears away, causing pain and swelling. Injuries to ligaments and tendons also cause knee problems
Symptoms:Swelling and stiffness,Redness,Weakness,crunching noises
Recovery:six weeks
Dietary Intake :Omega-3s,eat two 3-ounce servings of fatty fish each week, trout, salmon, mackerel, herring, tuna, and sardines


Finger Injury

More Details

Finger pain is common.Once you know the cause of your finger pain, you can determine what steps can be taken for the treatment of your condition.
Symptoms:including broken fingers and sprains, or chronic conditions, such as arthritis
Recovery:3 to 6 weeks
Dietary Intake :Carrots, spinach, sweet potatoes, and kale for vitamin A; oranges, strawberries, peppers, and broccoli for vitamin C


Thigh Injury

More Details

A thigh strain refers to an injury where the fibres in a quadriceps muscles are overstretched
Symptoms:Sudden and severe pain,Pain in the back of the thigh,Tenderness,Bruising
Recovery:up to six weeks or more
Dietary Intake :Omega-3 fatty acids,Extra virgin olive oil,Antioxidants, such as vitamin C, carotenes, and bioflavonoids,Vitamin D,Certain spices


Tennis Elbow

More Details

Tennis elbow is an inflammation of the tendons that join the forearm muscles on the outside of the elbow
Symptoms:The pain associated with tennis elbow may radiate from the outside of your elbow into your forearm and wrist
Recovery: 6 to 8 weeks
Dietary Intake :intake of fresh fruits and vegetables, nuts, whole grains and fatty fish, such as salmon


Ankle Injury

More Details

While ankle pain can result from a large number of ankle and foot injuries, the most common ankle injuries are sprains
Symptoms:swelling,tenderness,bruising,pain,skin discoloration,stiffness
Recovery:at least six weeks
Dietary Intake :meat and fish enhance the body's muscle-building process. Citrus fruits and dark leafy greens rich in vitamin C


Shoulder Injury

More Details

If your shoulder is pulled back too hard or rotated too far, the top of your arm might pop out of its socket
Symptoms:Pain at rest and at night,Pain when lifting and lowering your arm,Crepitus or crackling sensation
Recovery:about three to six months
Dietary Intake :onions, asparagus, brown rice and avocados. Consider avoiding foods that contain solanine, including eggplant, peppers and white potatoes
